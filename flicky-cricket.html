FLICKY CRICKET

Swipe up on the ball as it arrives to flick it!

Bigger / faster swipe = more power.
Time it right to find the gaps.
Miss it = bowled. Hit it soft to a fielder = caught.
A quick fielder can cut a four down to 1-2!
Hit hard against the ball's line and you'll mistime it (cross-bat)!
Big swings drain your energy bar - place some gentle shots to recover.
